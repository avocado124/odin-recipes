Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possible one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragu, bechamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses and seasonings and spices. The dish may be topped with grated cheese, which becomes melted after baking. Tyically cooked pasta is assembled with the other ingredients and then baked in a oven. The resulting baked pasta is cut into single-serving square portions.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garli
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
